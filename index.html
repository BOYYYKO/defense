The mission of our
organization

The mission of the "Defense of Ukrainian Society" is focused on supporting the military and ensuring societal security, promoting their development, and mobilizing the public. Our organization works for the benefit of Ukraine, providing protection, solidarity, and security for the entire society.

Goals and areas of the Civil
Society Organization

The main goal of the Foundation's activities is to carry out charitable activities for the benefit of society by uniting the efforts of the public aimed at facilitating the practical implementation of national, regional, local, and international programs aimed at improving the socio-economic situation in Ukraine and enhancing the material well-being of the beneficiaries of charitable assistance.

Cooperation with
military administrations

Cooperation between civil society organizations and military administrations is an important aspect of societal development and ensuring the security and well-being of the population. It is based on interaction and partnership between these two sectors, aimed at achieving common goals. Collaboration contributes to improving mutual understanding, joint efforts, and the achievement of shared objectives, which include security, humanitarian assistance, human rights protection, infrastructure development, and much more.

Our organization actively assists children who have lost their parents. Helping children is important and necessary to ensure their well-being, development, and integration into society. Housing Reconstruction
Project for Ukrainians

Together with our team, we are launching a new charitable program for Ukrainians who have lost their homes during the war. The war has displaced 13 million Ukrainians, and many of them have nowhere to return to as their homes have been turned into ruins by the Russian army. Rebuilding their homes is impossible. The government has been collecting information about the destroyed housing for a year through the mobile application "Diya." Ukrainians have submitted 350,000 applications. Today, we want to support not only our defenders but also provide assistance to our civilian population.

Military Rehabilitation
Project

The project of our organization on the rehabilitation of veterans is an important initiative aimed at supporting and integrating veterans and military personnel who have returned from combat or have post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and other service-related issues.
The main goal of the project is to provide comprehensive support and rehabilitation services to the military, helping them restore physical, psychological, and social well-being.

Ambassador of our organization

The Ambassador of our organization is John Sweany. His appointment in this role recognizes his significant contribution and dedication to the organization's cause. John Sweany is an outstanding individual known for his active stance and concern for the well-being of Ukraine. He is a prominent activist, leader of civic movements, and philanthropist. He brings attention to important issues regarding Ukraine's national security and support for the military. As an Ambassador of "Defense of Society of Ukraine," John Sweany utilizes his position and influence to raise awareness about the needs of the military and mobilize resources for their support. He actively collaborates with the goal of providing necessary assistance and resources to support the military and strengthen Ukraine's security.
